The recent boost achieved in computational power coupled with the advancement in mechanical designing tools and other semiconductor technologies have propelled the world to the zenith of Robotics and Automation innovation. I was first introduced to this field by a Youtube video of Boston Dynamic’s BigDog Robot, roughly six years ago. The control scheme of a robot is analogous to the neural sub-system of the human body and hence, it plays a significant role in assimilating the desired behaviour into the robot. I aspire to contribute to this scientific endeavour by innovating intelligent systems mimicking the decisive behaviour of humans efficiently in all conditions.

I am currently a Master of Science (M.S.) student in Electrical and Computer Engineering (E.C.E) at the University of California, San Diego. My specialization is in the field of Intelligent Systems, Robotics and Control.
