Hi !!

As a Localization System Engineer at XPENG, a leading Chinese electric autonomous vehicle company, I apply my expertise in intelligent systems, robotics, and control to estimate and predict the location of the car on an HD map. I have been working in this role for over a year, after completing my Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, San Diego. My main goal is to use several statistical techniques to fuse data provided by sensors like GNSS, IMU, camera, LIDAR, etc., fitted on the car. In the past I have worked with Robot Operating System (ROS), V-Rep and Gazebo to develop and test algorithms for object tracking, loop closure detection, and pose estimation. I am passionate about solving challenging problems and delivering innovative solutions for the future of mobility.
